PCBs:
    JCM632-AN1M:
        SOP-44 ROM, max 16Mbit
        SOP-28 SRAM, battery backed, max 64Kbit?
        Series resistors on ROM data pins (not SRAM)
        Pullup/pulldown resistor places for PB1 and PB3
        DET shorted to VCC
        /RESET weak pulldown, high enables SRAM with CS2
    Z544-1:
        Also known as A240427-1 (rev A) or A240427B-1 (rev B)
        2x SOP-44 ROM (max 32Mbit total)
        Single chip config: max 32Mbit
        Dual chip config: max 16+8Mbit
        SOP-28 SRAM, battery backed, max 64Kbit?
        Series resistors on data pins
        Pullup resistor place for PB3
        DET shorted to VCC
        Revision B adds a capacitor on /RD, unknown purpose
        Expansion audio (OPTIONAL):
            OKI MSM6653A-xxx (MCU type, samples in mask rom)
            PB1 = OKI RESET
            PB5 = OKI CMD
            PB7 = OKI NAR
            PB8 = OKI CH
            PB10 = OKI ST
            74HC273A latch (D0-D6)
            4.096MHz resonator
            BA15218 audio op amp
    Z545-1 PCB:
        Also known as A240570-1
        SOP-44 ROM (max 32Mbit, known games use 16Mbit)
        SOP-28 SRAM, battery backed, 64Kbit or 256Kbit
        Series resistors on ROM data pins (not SRAM)
        Pullup resistor places for PB1, PB3, PB15
        Jumper for DET to VCC or PB1
        /RESET 100K pulldown, high enables SRAM with CS2 or extra logic
    Z542 PCB set:
        Bespoke for Magical Shop
        Z542-1 lower board:
            SOP-44 ROM
            RH-7502 video capture ASIC
            TLC2932 PLL, probably used by ASIC for sample clock
        Z542-2 upper board:
            Cartridge passthrough
            CXA1228S NTSC/PAL to YPbPr decoder (wired for NTSC)
            Audio buffer/mixer for input and passthrough audio
